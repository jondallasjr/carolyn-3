One-Time Costs

Arrival Setup

  • Cell phone plan: $0–$30
  • Initial groceries: $50–$100
  • Deposits/initial services: $100–$200

Visa & Documentation

  • DTV Application: $340
  • Medical consultation: $40–$90
  • 30-day tourist extension: $58 (1,900 THB)

Medical

  • Initial consultation: $40–$90
  • Medical records compilation: $0–$100

Budget Tips

  • ATM fees: Expect 220 THB (~$6.50) per withdrawal. Withdraw larger amounts to minimize fees.
  • Cleaning services: 500–800 THB ($15–$24) for 2–3 hours. Much cheaper than US.
  • Transportation: Grab is inexpensive. Motorbike rentals: $4–$8/day.
  • Healthcare: Bangkok Hospital and private clinics have English-speaking staff and lower costs than US.
  • Food: Street food and local restaurants are cheapest. Western groceries at specialty stores cost more.
  • Social activities: Many expat groups and activities are free or low-cost.
  • US disability income: Likely stretches much further in Thailand than in the US.
