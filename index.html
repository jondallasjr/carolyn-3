Quick Facts

  • DTV Visa Duration: 5 years
  • Initial Entry: 60-day visa exemption (US citizens)
  • Cost Savings: Physical therapy $25-75/session vs $100+ in US
  • Housing: $250-500/month in Suthep area
  • Financial Requirement: 500,000+ THB ($15,000+ USD) for sponsor
  • Processing Time: 2 days to 2 weeks for DTV approval
