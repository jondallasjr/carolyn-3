Critical Information

Visa Exemption First

US citizens get automatic 60-day visa exemption on arrival. No visa needed for initial entry.

Medical Records Required

Bring US medical records: surgeries, MRI, X-rays. Essential for DTV approval and treatment plan.

DTV Application from Vietnam

Must apply from outside Thailand (Vietnam recommended). Cannot apply once in Thailand.

High Approval Rate

Very high DTV approval rates from Hanoi and Ho Chi Minh City embassies. Online-only since Oct 2024.

Financial Sponsor

Need sponsor with $15,000+ in 3+ months bank statements and sponsorship letter.

Medication Availability

Most US medications available in Thailand at fraction of US cost. Verify with pharmacist.
