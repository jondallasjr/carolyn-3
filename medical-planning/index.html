Medical Plan & DTV Foundation

Establishing medical consultation with Thai doctors to build the foundation for DTV visa approval.

Overview

The medical consultation and plan is a critical requirement for DTV visa approval. This phase involves scheduling an in-person consultation with a Thai doctor, reviewing your medical history, and establishing a formal medical plan for ongoing treatment.

Timeline & Key Dates

Sep 16 - Oct 14

Get a Medical Plan (required for DTV)

  • Contact Bangkok Hospital in Chiang Mai
  • Schedule in-person consultation (~$40-90)
  • Review medical records with Thai doctor
  • Get a 6 month+ medical plan for ongoing PT/pain management
Key Notes:
  • This consultation creates the foundation for visa approval
  • Bangkok Hospital has proven track record for DTV medical letters
  • Must show 6+ months treatment needed
  • Physical therapy costs $25-75/session vs $100+ in US

Why This Matters for DTV

The DTV (Digital Nomad Visa) requires documented medical justification for your stay. The medical consultation accomplishes three critical things:

  • Creates Medical Documentation: Thai doctors provide official letters confirming your medical condition and need for treatment
  • Establishes Treatment Plan: A formal 6+ month treatment plan demonstrates commitment and justifies the visa duration
  • Builds Trust: Working with established Thai medical facilities (like Bangkok Hospital) strengthens your visa application credibility

What to Bring to Consultation

  • US medical records (surgeries, MRI, X-rays, etc.)
  • Current medication list with dosages
  • Medical history documentation
  • Insurance information
  • Passport for identification

Next Steps

  1. Contact Bangkok Hospital: Reach out via phone or online to schedule consultation
  2. Prepare Documents: Organize all medical records from US doctors
  3. Schedule Appointment: Book consultation within first 3 weeks of arrival
  4. Attend Consultation: Bring all documents and discuss 6+ month treatment plan
  5. Obtain Medical Letters: Request formal documentation for DTV application
  6. Begin Treatment: Start physical therapy or ongoing medical care
