Phase 5: DTV Application & Vietnam Process

6 weeks | Compiling complete application, traveling to Vietnam, waiting for approval, and returning to Thailand with approved DTV

Overview

This final phase transforms you from a tourist into a long-term resident with a 5-year visa. You'll compile your complete application package, travel to Vietnam for the application process, and return with approval.

Key Milestones

Week 1-3: Application Prep

Compile all documents: medical letters, financial sponsor paperwork, passport copies, and medical records.

Week 2-4: Vietnam Travel

Apply for Vietnamese visa, book accommodation, and prepare for 1-2 week stay while DTV processes.

Week 3-5: Application Submit

Submit DTV application online within 24 hours of arrival in Vietnam. Pay $340 non-refundable fee.

Week 4-6: Approval & Return

Wait for approval (2 days to 2 weeks), receive DTV electronically, return to Thailand with approval.

Detailed Task Breakdown

DTV Application Preparation (Weeks 1-3)

Compile Complete Document Package

  • Medical plan and letters from Bangkok Hospital Chiang Mai
  • Financial Sponsor documents:
    • Bank statements showing 500,000+ THB ($15,000+ USD) for 3+ months
    • Sponsorship letter committing to financial support for emergencies (flight home)
    • Proof of relationship (birth certificate)
  • Passport photos (multiple copies)
  • Copies of all US medical records
  • Proof of accommodation in Thailand

Critical: All documents must be error-free. Typos cause automatic rejection.

Prepare for Short Stay in Vietnam (Weeks 2-4)

Vietnamese Visa & Accommodation

  • Get Vietnamese tourist visa ($25 online, or visa-on-arrival)
  • Book accommodation in Vietnam ($30-40/night)
  • Budget $400-600 total for Vietnam stay
  • Plan for 1-2 week stay during processing

Important: Cannot return to Thailand during DTV processing. Must wait in Vietnam.

Apply for DTV (Week 3)

Submit Online Application

  • Submit online application at thaievisa.go.th within 24 hours of arrival in Vietnam
  • Treatment Justification example: "Physical therapy too expensive in US, affordable in Thailand"
  • Pay non-refundable application fee: $340

Note: Must apply from outside Thailand (cannot apply from inside Thailand). Online-only applications since October 2024.

Embassy Selection

  • Hanoi or Ho Chi Minh City embassies have VERY HIGH DTV approval rates
  • Process time: 5 working days officially, but 2 days to 2 weeks realistically

Wait for Approval & Process Documents (Weeks 4-5)

During Processing

  • Approval arrives via email electronically
  • Embassy may request additional documents (have copies ready)
  • Cannot return to Thailand during this time

When Approved

  • Receive DTV electronically
  • Print multiple copies of approval (at least 3-5)
  • Prepare for return travel

Return to Thailand with DTV (Week 6)

Border Entry

  • Fly back to Chiang Mai (or any Thailand entry point) with approved DTV
  • Show printed DTV approval at border
  • Stay for 180 days (or extend for up to 360 days with one extension)

Success! No re-entry permits needed for 5 years. This solves your visa issues for 5+ years.

Critical Information

DTV Approval Rate

Very high from Hanoi and Ho Chi Minh City embassies. Success depends mainly on complete, error-free documentation.

Processing Time

Official: 5 working days
Realistic: 2 days to 2 weeks depending on embassy workload

Financial Sponsor Requirements

Must show 500,000+ THB ($15,000+ USD) in bank account for 3+ months continuously.

Stay Duration

Can stay continuously for up to 360 days with one extension (~$300). Or do border run every 180 days for fresh entry.

Week-by-Week Timeline

1

Weeks 1-3: Document Compilation

Gather all required documents from Bangkok Hospital, financial sponsor, and government offices. Ensure everything is error-free and properly formatted.

2

Week 2: Book Vietnam Travel

Apply for Vietnamese visa if needed. Book flights and accommodation in Hanoi or Ho Chi Minh City for 1-2 week stay.

3

Week 3: Travel to Vietnam

Fly from Chiang Mai to Vietnam. Upon arrival, immediately submit DTV application online at thaievisa.go.th within 24 hours.

4

Weeks 4-5: Wait for Approval

Wait in Vietnam while embassy processes application. This typically takes 2-14 days. Embassy will email approval when ready.

5

Week 6: Return to Thailand

Print DTV approval, book return flight to Thailand. Show approval at border to activate your 5-year DTV visa.

Estimated Costs

DTV Application Fee $340
Vietnamese Visa $25
Vietnam Accommodation (7-14 nights) $210-560
Flights (Chiang Mai↔Vietnam) $100-300
Total Phase 5 $675-1,225

After Approval: Long-Term Stay

Once you have your 5-year DTV visa:

  • Can stay continuously for up to 360 days with one extension (~$300)
  • Or exit and re-enter Thailand every 180 days for fresh entry
  • No longer need to worry about visa runs or visa exemptions
  • Can focus on your physical therapy, recovery, and settling into Chiang Mai life
  • Continue your medical treatment plan with Bangkok Hospital
  • Build deeper connections in expat and local communities
