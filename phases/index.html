Your Thailand Journey: 5 Phases

A comprehensive roadmap from US to 5-year DTV visa in Thailand

Phase 1

Pre-Departure Preparation

7-8 weeks before departure

Gather essential documents, medical records, research communities, arrange travel insurance, and prepare for your move.

  • Collect medical records & imaging
  • Book flights and accommodation
  • Research Chiang Mai & expat communities
  • Arrange travel health insurance
  • Organize possessions & finances
View Phase Details →
Phase 2

Travel to Thailand

24-25 hours travel time

Travel from Baltimore to Chiang Mai with layovers. Enter on 60-day visa exemption, setup phone service, and settle into first accommodation.

  • Multi-leg flight: Baltimore → Chiang Mai
  • 60-day visa exemption entry
  • Airport phone setup
  • Transportation to accommodation
  • Basic orientation & rest
View Phase Details →
Phase 3

Initial Settling In

3 weeks after arrival

Establish core services, connect with recovery community, set up basic routines, and begin exploring Chiang Mai.

  • Find primary care doctor & PT
  • Join AA & recovery groups
  • Setup grocery & transportation
  • Establish cleaning & daily services
  • Begin adjustment & exploration
View Phase Details →
Phase 4

Medical Planning for DTV

4 weeks in country

Establish medical treatment plan with Thai doctors, get required letters, plan lifestyle activities, and compile DTV application documents.

  • Consult with Bangkok Hospital doctors
  • Create 6+ month medical plan
  • Obtain medical treatment letters
  • Join activity groups & communities
  • Prepare application documents
View Phase Details →
Phase 5

DTV Application & Vietnam Process

6 weeks total process

Compile complete application package, travel to Vietnam for submission, wait for approval, and return to Thailand with your 5-year visa.

  • Finalize all application documents
  • Travel to Vietnam (Hanoi/HCMC)
  • Submit DTV application
  • Wait for approval (2-14 days)
  • Return to Thailand with DTV

Key Information

Total Timeline

Approximately 4 months from decision to approved DTV in hand

Initial Visa

60-day visa exemption automatically upon arrival (US citizens)

DTV Duration

5 years once approved, with annual extensions available

Stay Duration

Up to 180-360 days per visit with border runs or extensions
