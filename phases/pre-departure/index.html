Phase 1: Pre-Departure Preparation

4-6 weeks before departure. Essential tasks including document gathering, travel insurance, housing research, and medical records preparation.

1

Pre-Departure Preparation

July 16 – August 23, 2025 (6 weeks)

Essential Travel Planning

  • Buy flight
  • Book temporary accommodation for first 2 weeks
  • Arrange travel health insurance

Get Rid of Things

  • Sell/donate/pack possessions
  • Sell/store car

Gather Documents

  • US medical records (surgeries, MRI, X-rays, etc.)
  • Passport
  • Emergency contact list (doctor, hospital, Jon, US family)

Research

  • Join Chiang Mai Expats Facebook groups
  • Specific responsibilities of a Financial Sponsor
  • Research prescription medication availability in Thailand
  • Create Thailand budget based on actual costs vs US disability income
  • Identify 24/7 English-speaking medical facilities
  • Plan for burning season – potential southern Thailand/Bali trip during worst air quality months

Locate Nearby Amenities in Suthep Area

  • Convenience store
  • Grocery store
  • Restaurants
  • AA Meetings
  • Counseling centers, Art Therapy, etc.
  • Gym and swimming pool
  • Physical Therapist
  • Hospital

Key Notes

  • No visa needed: US citizens get 60-day visa exemption automatically.
  • Medical records essential for DTV later.
  • Many US medications available in Thailand at fraction of cost.
  • Suthep area has large expat community with English-speaking services.
