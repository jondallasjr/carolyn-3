Key Information

  • Visa: No need to apply for visa - US citizens get 60-day visa exemption automatically.
  • Medications: Many US medications available in Thailand at a fraction of US cost.
  • Location: Suthep area has large expat community with English-speaking services.
  • Timeline: Plan for 6-8 weeks of preparation before departure.
