Resources & Links

Curated directory of useful links, contacts, and organizations for Thailand relocation

Visa & Immigration

  • Thai e-Visa Portal (thaievisa.go.th) - Official DTV application platform
  • Thai Immigration Bureau - Official immigration information
  • Thai Ministry of Foreign Affairs - Embassy and consulate locator
  • US Embassy Bangkok - For US citizens requiring consular services

Medical Services in Chiang Mai

  • Bangkok Hospital Chiang Mai - English-speaking medical facility, DTV medical letters
  • Dr. Morgan, Health Care Medical Clinic - Recommended primary care physician
  • Physical therapy clinics throughout Suthep area
  • International pharmacies with English-speaking staff

Housing & Accommodation

  • Airbnb - Short-term rentals
  • Booking.com - Hotels and accommodations
  • Facebook groups: "Chiang Mai Housing", "Suthep Rentals"
  • Local real estate agents in Chiang Mai - Can arrange long-term rentals ($250-500/month in Suthep)

Recovery & Community

  • AA Meetings Chiang Mai - 50+ meetings per week throughout the city
  • Refuge Recovery - Buddhist-based recovery program
  • Counselling Thailand - Addiction counseling services
  • The Cabin Alumni Network - Support for treatment program graduates
  • Chiang Mai Expats Facebook groups - Social and activity communities

Transportation & Local Services

  • Grab App - Taxis, motorbikes, and ride-sharing
  • Local SIM cards available at Chiang Mai Airport
  • AIS, DTAC, True Move - Thai mobile providers with English support
  • Local convenience stores (7-Eleven, Family Mart)
  • Grocery stores and markets in Suthep neighborhood

Activities & Recreation

  • Gym and pool memberships - Available throughout Chiang Mai ($30-100/month)
  • Yoga studios and classes
  • Hiking groups and outdoor activities
  • Art therapy and creative workshops
  • Cooking classes and food tours
  • Board game groups and meetups

Financial & Banking

  • Thai banks: Kasikornbank (K-Bank), Bangkok Bank, Krung Thai Bank
  • ATMs throughout Chiang Mai (220 THB fee for foreign cards)
  • Exchange services for currency conversion
  • International wire transfer services

Useful Information

  • Currency: Thai Baht (THB) - Approximately $1 USD = 33-35 THB
  • Emergency Services: 911 (same as US)
  • Time Zone: Indochina Time (ICT), UTC+7
  • Language: Thai language apps and classes available
  • Burning Season: March-May (consider southern Thailand or Bali during worst air quality months)

Tips for Using These Resources

  • Join Chiang Mai Expats Facebook groups before arrival to connect with the community
  • Many services have English-speaking staff in the Suthep area
  • Costs are significantly lower than US - verify with local contacts for current pricing
  • Building relationships with local service providers (doctors, therapists) is essential for DTV medical documentation
  • Always keep copies of medical records and important documents
