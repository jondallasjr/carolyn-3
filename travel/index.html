Arrival Process

1. Immigration & Visa Exemption

US citizens receive automatic 60-day visa exemption upon arrival. No visa required.

You will receive an automatic 60-day stamp in your passport at immigration.

2. Collect Luggage & Customs

Standard baggage collection and customs procedures. Plan for 30-60 minutes.

3. Get a Cell Phone Plan

Purchase a local Thai SIM card at the airport. Major providers: AIS, Dtac, True Move.

Cost: 99-299 THB ($3-9) for starter plans with data

4. Currency Exchange

Exchange USD to Thai Baht at airport money changer or ATM. Airport rates are reasonable.

Note: ATM withdrawals charge 220 THB ($6.50) per transaction‚Äîwithdraw larger amounts.

5. Transportation to Accommodation

Download Grab app (Thai Uber equivalent) or take an airport taxi.

Cost: 80-150 THB ($2-4.50) to Suthep area via Grab
