Thailand DTV Visa Guide

Complete roadmap to obtaining the 5-year Destination Thailand Visa for medical tourism and long-term stays

What is the DTV Visa?

Duration

5 years of continuous stay in Thailand

Purpose

Medical tourism, wellness, and treatment in Thailand

Application Fee

$340 USD non-refundable

Requirements

Medical plan, financial sponsor, documentation

Key Requirements

Medical Documentation

  • Medical plan from Thai hospital (6+ months treatment)
  • Consultation with Thai doctor (~$40-90)
  • US medical records (surgeries, MRI, X-rays, etc.)
  • Proof of ongoing treatment need

Financial Sponsor

  • Bank statements: 500,000+ THB ($15,000+ USD)
  • Funds held for 3+ months
  • Sponsorship letter committing to emergency support
  • Proof of relationship to applicant

Personal Documents

  • Valid passport
  • Passport photos
  • Visa application form
  • Emergency contact information

Application Rules

  • Must apply from OUTSIDE Thailand
  • Online-only applications (since Oct 2024)
  • Submit within 24 hours of arrival in Vietnam
  • All documents must be error-free

DTV Application Timeline

1

Pre-Departure (7-8 weeks before)

Gather documents, research, book temporary accommodation, arrange travel insurance

2

Arrival in Thailand (60-day visa exemption)

No visa required for US citizens. Settle in, establish with doctors and services

3

Medical Planning (weeks 3-5)

Consult Bangkok Hospital or Thai doctor, create 6-month medical treatment plan

4

Optional 30-Day Extension (week 7)

Extend tourist stay to 90 days at immigration office (1,900 THB)

5

Document Compilation (weeks 7-8)

Gather medical letters, financial documents, sponsor information, passport photos

6

Travel to Vietnam (week 8)

Fly to Hanoi or Ho Chi Minh City. Must leave Thailand to submit application

7

Submit Application & Wait (day 1 in Vietnam)

Submit online at thaievisa.go.th. Official processing: 5 working days. Realistic: 2 days to 2 weeks

8

Return to Thailand with DTV

Receive approval via email. Print multiple copies. Show at border. Valid for 5 years!

DTV Approval Success Rate

VERY HIGH approval rate from Hanoi and Ho Chi Minh City embassies

Key factors for approval:

  • Complete medical documentation showing 6+ months treatment need
  • Clear financial sponsor backing with sufficient funds
  • Error-free document submission (typos cause automatic rejection)
  • Proof of actual medical treatment plan from Thai hospital

Important Notes

⚠️ Cannot Apply from Inside Thailand

You MUST leave Thailand to submit your application. Vietnam trip is required.

⚠️ Cannot Return During Processing

You must stay in Vietnam while waiting for approval. Returning to Thailand voids your application.

✅ No Re-entry Permit Needed

With DTV approval, you can leave and return to Thailand for 5 years without additional visas.

✅ Extended Stay Option

You can stay continuously for up to 360 days with one extension (~$300). Or do a border run every 180 days.

Common Questions

Can US citizens get a 60-day visa exemption?

Yes! US citizens automatically receive a 60-day tourist stamp upon arrival in Thailand. No visa needed beforehand.

How much does the DTV cost?

$340 USD application fee, plus costs for medical consultation (~$40-90), documentation gathering, and Vietnam trip ($400-600).

What if my application is rejected?

Very rare with proper documentation. Most rejections are due to typos or incomplete medical justification. The $340 fee is non-refundable.

How long does approval take?

Official timeline: 5 working days. Realistic: 2 days to 2 weeks. You'll receive approval via email.
