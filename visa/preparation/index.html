Critical Requirements

❌ Common Rejection Reasons

  • Typos or spelling errors in documents
  • Inconsistent information across forms
  • Medical plan less than 6 months
  • Sponsor funds below 500,000 THB
  • Missing signatures or dates
  • Poor quality document scans

⚠️ Important Notes

  • ALL documents must be error-free
  • Double-check every figure and date
  • Print multiple copies of all approvals
  • Cannot return to Thailand during processing
  • Stay in Vietnam while waiting
  • Embassy may request additional docs

✓ Success Factors

  • Very high approval rates from Hanoi & HCMC
  • Medical plan from established hospital
  • Complete, well-organized documents
  • Clear treatment justification
  • Sponsor with strong financial backing
  • Multiple copies of everything

Preparation Timeline

Phase 1: Medical Setup (Sept 16 - Oct 14)

Schedule consultation with Bangkok Hospital Chiang Mai. Get 6-month medical plan and treatment letters. Cost: $40-90 for consultation.

Phase 2: Document Compilation (Oct 15 - Nov 8)

Gather all documents from sponsor. Verify financial statements show 500K+ THB. Get relationship proof. Prepare all copies.

Phase 3: Final Review (Nov 9 - Nov 10)

Triple-check all documents for errors. Verify all signatures and dates. Make multiple high-quality copies. Organize packet.

Phase 4: Vietnam Travel (Nov 11 - Nov 12)

Travel to Hanoi or Ho Chi Minh City with complete document package and Financial Sponsor if possible.

Application Fee & Logistics

Non-Refundable Application Fee

$340 USD

Submitted online at thaievisa.go.th within 24 hours of arrival in Vietnam

Submission Method

Online Only

Since October 2024, all DTV applications must be submitted online. No in-person applications accepted.

Processing Location

Vietnamese Embassy

Hanoi or Ho Chi Minh City. High approval rates from both locations.

Financial Sponsor Requirements

Who Can Be a Sponsor?

Typically a close family member (parent, spouse, adult child) who is willing to financially support you in case of emergency.

Sponsor Responsibilities

  • Maintain 500,000+ THB (~$15,000+ USD) in bank account for 3+ months
  • Provide signed sponsorship letter committing to emergency support
  • Provide bank statements as proof
  • Provide proof of relationship (birth certificate)
  • Agree to pay for emergency return flight if needed

Documentation Timeline

CRITICAL: Sponsor must have 500K+ THB in account for at least 3 months BEFORE application. Start collecting funds by early August at the latest.

Next Steps

Once your document package is complete and verified:

  1. Travel to Vietnam with your Financial Sponsor (Nov 11-12)
  2. Submit online application at thaievisa.go.th within 24 hours (Nov 12)
  3. Wait for approval - stays in Vietnam during processing (Nov 12-19)
  4. Return to Thailand with approved DTV visa (Nov 19+)
  5. Enjoy 5-year stay in Thailand
