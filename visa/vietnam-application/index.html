Vietnam Application & Processing

Submit your DTV application in Vietnam and wait for approval while exploring the country

Why Vietnam?

High Approval Rate

Hanoi and Ho Chi Minh City embassies have very high DTV approval rates

Cannot Apply from Thailand

DTV applications must be submitted from outside Thailand only

Affordable Stay

$30-40/night accommodation while waiting for approval

Fast Processing

Official timeline: 5 working days. Realistic: 2 days to 2 weeks

Vietnam Trip Timeline

1

Travel to Vietnam

Fly from Chiang Mai to Hanoi or Ho Chi Minh City with your financial sponsor (Jon). Bring all DTV application documents.

2

Submit Application

Submit complete DTV application package at Thai Embassy within 24 hours of arrival for best processing time.

3

Wait in Vietnam

Stay in Vietnam during processing. Cannot return to Thailand until approval received via email.

4

Receive Approval

Print multiple copies of DTV approval. Embassy may request additional documents during this period.

5

Return to Thailand

Fly back to Chiang Mai with approved DTV visa. Show printed approval at border. 180-day stay begins.

Vietnam Entry Requirements

Vietnamese Tourist Visa

  • US citizens need visa for Vietnam ($25 online)
  • Can obtain e-visa online before travel
  • Valid for 30 days single entry
  • Processing: 3-5 business days

Budget for Vietnam Stay

  • Accommodation: $30-40/night
  • Food & transport: $15-25/day
  • Total estimated: $400-600 for 7-14 day stay
  • Embassy may request additional docs during wait

Documents to Bring

  • Printed DTV application package (multiple copies)
  • Passport
  • Medical plan letters from doctors
  • Financial sponsor documentation

Critical Timing

  • Submit within 24 hours of arrival for best processing
  • Official processing: 5 working days
  • Realistic timeline: 2 days to 2 weeks
  • Check email daily for approval or requests

Embassy Selection

VERY HIGH approval rate from Hanoi and Ho Chi Minh City

Both embassies have proven track records with DTV applications. Choose based on:

  • Hanoi Embassy: Northern route, slightly larger city, well-established expat network
  • Ho Chi Minh City Embassy: Southern route, larger city, robust infrastructure
  • Both process applications efficiently with clear procedures
  • Both have experience with medical tourism DTV applications

Pro tip: Research current wait times for your chosen embassy before booking flights.

Critical Information

🚫 Cannot Return During Processing

You must stay in Vietnam while waiting for DTV approval. Do not return to Thailand or your application may be denied.

📧 Check Email Regularly

Approval arrives via email with DTV document. Print multiple copies immediately. Embassy may also request additional documents via email.

✓ Bring Financial Sponsor

It's recommended (and often required) to travel with your financial sponsor (Jon) for the application submission.

📋 Document Perfection

All documents must be error-free. Typos or inconsistencies cause automatic rejection. Review thoroughly before submission.

💰 Additional Requests

Embassy may request additional financial documents or clarifications during processing. Budget extra time if this occurs.

🛂 Border Entry Procedure

When returning to Thailand with approved DTV, show printed approval at immigration. 5-year visa and 180-day entry permit issued immediately.

Vietnam Trip Checklist

Before You Leave Thailand

  • Obtain Vietnamese tourist e-visa ($25)
  • Book return flights to Thailand
  • Verify all DTV documents are error-free
  • Print multiple copies of entire application package
  • Confirm financial sponsor will travel with you

Upon Arrival in Vietnam

  • Go to Thai Embassy within 24 hours
  • Submit complete DTV application package
  • Receive submission receipt and timeline
  • Get embassy contact info for inquiries
  • Ask about email notification procedure

While Waiting (2-14 days)

  • Explore Vietnam responsibly
  • Check email multiple times daily
  • Respond immediately to any embassy requests
  • Keep printed copies accessible at all times
  • Do not leave Vietnam during processing

Upon Approval

  • Print multiple copies of DTV approval email
  • Book flight back to Chiang Mai
  • Pack printed approvals in carry-on bag
  • Arrive at Thailand border prepared
  • Present approval document to immigration

What Happens After Approval

Once your DTV visa is approved and you return to Thailand, you'll have a 5-year visa with:

  • 180-day continuous stay: You can stay in Thailand for up to 180 days without leaving
  • No re-entry permits needed: For the full 5 years, you don't need special permits to stay
  • Extension option: Can extend for additional 180 days (costs ~$300) or do border runs every 180 days
  • Long-term stability: This visa solves your visa situation for 5+ years, allowing you to focus on settling and treatment

Your DTV approval opens the door to deep roots in Thailand—whether that's permanent housing in Suthep, ongoing medical treatment, building community, or long-term recovery support.
